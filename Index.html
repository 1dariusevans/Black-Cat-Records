Black Cat Records

Posted by Darius Evans

Black Cat is a fictional streaming service that gives the audience the power to control the music charts all from their smart devices.

How does it work?

You start by subscribing for $4.99. Then go and listen to your favorite artists. Vote for your favorite songs on Black Cat Records App via Viewer's Choice Charts. The more you listen to music the more views you're eligible.
